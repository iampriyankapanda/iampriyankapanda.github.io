Welcome to SKI NOW

We’re the leading provider of fully catered halal ski and chalet holidays.

We work with chalet owners across Europe to offer you a truly memorable and bespoke ski experience, catering to all your needs.

Our chalets are perfect for families, groups of friends and couples.

Our aim is simple, help you to choose the most suitable resort for you, you’re family and friends – whether you’re a beginner or expert skier.

We listen to your requirements and then arrange everything you need for an unforgettable adventure.

We love skiing and snowboarding, we want you to share this amazing feeling that we share, please browse our site and find out why.
